Welcome To Dajiho Lab

Dajiho Lab is a Lagos-based R&D company conducting basic and applied research in the areas of Extended Reality(VR, AR & MR), Artificial Intelligence and Computer Music.

We are a team of polymaths from many disciplines, coming from as many different fields as Computer Science, Mathematics, Medicine, Music, Fashion Design, Cognitive Science, Psychology, etc. .
